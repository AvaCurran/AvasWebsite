Get to Know Me

I’m Ava Curran, a child development major and teacher who believes in hands-on learning. When I’m not in the classroom, you can find me exploring new cultures, tasting unique dishes, and collecting memories from around the globe.

My recent trip to Spain expanded my horizons and fueled my passion for traveling even more. Join me as I continue to discover what the world has to offer—and share those experiences both in the classroom and with fellow adventurers.
